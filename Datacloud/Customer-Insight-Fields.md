# Customer Insight Fields

The following customer insight fields are used or planned in NexaAssist.

| Field Name | Purpose |
|------------|----------|
| Customer Segment | Identifies VIP, Standard, At Risk, Enterprise, Trial, etc. |
| Health Score | Indicates overall customer health from 0–100 |
| Churn Risk | Indicates Low / Medium / High risk of customer churn |
| Preferred Support Channel | Stores whether the customer prefers Email, Chat, Phone, or Human Support |
| Adoption Score | Indicates how actively the customer is using the product |
| Lifetime Value | Represents estimated customer business value |
| VIP Customer Flag | Allows priority handling for strategic customers |
| Last Escalation Date | Helps identify repeated support issues |
| Open Case Count | Helps determine if the customer already has multiple active issues |
| Recommended Next Action | Used for future AI-based support recommendations |
